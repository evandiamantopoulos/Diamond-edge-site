How It Works

A streamlined pipeline that keeps quality and control with your firm.

1

Ingest

Drop in contracts, pleadings, policies, transcripts, or PDFs. Files are stored privately and never used to train third party models.

2

Index

We parse, chunk, and embed the text, then index it with jurisdiction and matter metadata for precise retrieval.

3

Retrieve

Queries pull only the most relevant passages from your library and trusted sources with link back citations.

4

Draft

Generate clean drafts, redlines, and memos with inline references. Every statement maps to a source.

5

Review

Attorney in the loop review completes the workflow with approvals, export to DOCX, and partner ready summaries.
